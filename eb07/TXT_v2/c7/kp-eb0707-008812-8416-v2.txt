COLOGNA, a city of the delegation of Verona, in the Austrian kingdom of Venetian Lombardy. It is on the canal of Frasene, and is a place of much trade in silk and its other products. It contains 3550 inhabitants. Long. 12. 13. 6. E. Lat. 45. 18. 11. N.
