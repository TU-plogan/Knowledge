GERTOKH, a town of Thibet, and a mart for the exchange of the productions of Nepaul with those of the country. The imports consist of grain, oil, sugar, cottons, chintzes, iron, brass, lead, dates, almonds, peaches, &c.; and from Gertokh are exported for the Cashmerian market, shawl wool, to Nepaul and Hindustan gold dust, silver, musk, scented leather, shawls, China ware, tea, salt, borax, &c.
