AREVALO, a town of Spain, in Old Castile, situated at the junction of the river Adaja with the Arevalillo. Lat. 41. 3. 57. N. and long. 4. 7. 57. W. It contains two magazines for corn, and has 1600 houses and 4500 inhabitants.
