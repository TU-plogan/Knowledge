ARMENTIERES, a small handsome town in the department of the North, in France, containing 7600 inhabitants. It was taken by Louis XIV. in 1667, who dismantled it. The river Lys traverses the town, and forms a small harbour. Long. 3. 3. E. Lat. 50. 40. N.
