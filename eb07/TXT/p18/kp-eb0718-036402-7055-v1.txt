POONAR, a town and district of Hindustan, in the province of Berar, and territory of the nizam. The district is mountainous and woody, and is situated between the twenty-first and twenty-second degrees of north latitude. The town is seventy-five miles south from Gluckpoor. Long. 78. 13. E. Lat. 20. 9. N.
