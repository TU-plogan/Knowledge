PUNDA, or Pundy, a town of Hindustan, province of Bejapore, twenty-five miles south-east from Goa, and near the south-west extremity of the territories belonging to the Poonah Mahrattas. Long. 74. 3. E. Lat. 15. 20. N. There is another place of the same name in the Northern Circars. Long. 84. 40. E. Lat. 18. 43. N.
