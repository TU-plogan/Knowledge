ALTERN-BASE, in Trigonometry, a term used in contradistinction to the true base. Thus, in oblique triangles, the true base is either the sum of the sides, and then the difference of the sides is called the altem-base; or the true base is the difference of the sides, and the sumoÄ© the sides is called the altern-base.
