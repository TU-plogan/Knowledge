ALGIDUM, a town of Latium, in Italy, between ľre-neste and Alba, near the mountains. On the top of one of these mountains was erected a temple of Diana, to which Horace refers, lib. i. ode 21, “Quaecunqw ŪU gelido prominet Algido ;” and lib. iii. ode 23, “Qua mvaii pascitur Algido,” &c.
