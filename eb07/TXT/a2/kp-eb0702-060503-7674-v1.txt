AMEND, or Amende, in the Freneh customs, a pecuniary punishment imposed by a judge for any erime, false prosecution, or groundless appeal.

AMENDE Honorable, a speeies of punishment formerly inflieted in Franee upon traitors, parrieides, or saerile-gious persons, in the following manner; The offender being delivered into the hands of the hangman, his shirt is stripped off, a rope put about his neek, and a taper plaeed in his hand; he is then led into eourt, where he must beg pardon of God, the king, the eourt, and his country.

Amende Honorable is a term also used for making recantation in open eourt, or in presence of the person injured.
