ACHAFALAYA, a river in Louisiana, in North America, or more properly a secondary channel of the Mississippi, by which a part of its waters flows off from the main trunk, and passes to the Gulf of Mexico. Its mouth is about 100 miles westward of the proper mouth of the Mississippi."
