ADRIPALDA, a city in the province of Principato Ulteriore, in the kingdom of Naples. It is situated on the river Sabato, near to Avellino. The number of inhabitants is 4236, who carry on trade in cloth and paper, in iron and copper goods, and make large quantities of nails.
