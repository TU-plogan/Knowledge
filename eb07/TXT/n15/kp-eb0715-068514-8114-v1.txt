NADONE, or Nadoun, a town of Hindustan, in the province of Lahore, being the principal one of the Kangrah country, is situated on the eastern bank of the Beyah river. This place was considered as a respectable fortress in the year 1014, and was taken from the Hindus by Sultan Mahmoud of Ghizni. The Nadone district borders on the Punjab.
