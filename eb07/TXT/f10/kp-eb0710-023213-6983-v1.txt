FRIEDBERG, a bailiwick in the circle of the Upper Danube, in the kingdom of Bavaria, extending over 178 square miles. It contains one city, eight towns, and 111 villages and hamlets, with 14,949 inhabitants. The capital, a city of the same name, stands on the Acha, with 330 houses, and 1871 inhabitants, among whom are watchmakers and ribbon weavers.
