BURHAMPOOR, a town of Hindustan, in the province of Bengal, situated on the east bank of Bhagirathi or Cossimbazar river, five miles from Moorshedabad. Here is stationed a brigade of troops in commodious cantonments, [7:5:712] which consist of a fine range of buildings in an open lawn. Long. 89. 14. E. Lat. 24. 3. N.
