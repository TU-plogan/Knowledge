BOROUGHBRIDGE, a borough and market-town in the parish of Aldborough and west-riding of Yorkshire, 208 miles from London, on the river Eyne, which is navigable to this place. The market is held on a Saturday. The number of inhabitants in 1801 amounted to 680, in 1811 to 745, and in 1821 to 860.
