KADMONAeI, or CadmonaeÄ©, in Ancient Geography, a people of Palestine, said to have dwelt at the foot of Mount Hermon, which lies to the eastward, and is the reason of the appellation, with respect to Libanus, Phoenicia, and the northern parts of Palestine. They were also called Hevoei.
