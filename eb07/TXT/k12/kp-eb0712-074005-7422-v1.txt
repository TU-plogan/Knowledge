KISCHENAU, a town of the Russian province of Bessarabia, the capital of a circle of the same name, and the see of a bishop of Bessarabia and Moldavia. It has an ecclesiastical seminary of the Greek church, and about 3500 inhabitants, among whom are many Jews. Long. 29. 2. 55. E. Lat. 46. 59. 30. N.
