KECSKEMET, a large town of the circle of Pest, in the Austrian kingdom of Hungary. It is in the middle of an extensive heath, between the Danube and the Theiss, and contains one reformed and four Catholic churehes, three colleges, a Franeisean convent, a military aeademy, 3000 houses, and 24,862 inhabitants, who earry on considerable trade in internal productions. Long. 19. 37. 6. E. Lat. 46. 54. 29. N.
