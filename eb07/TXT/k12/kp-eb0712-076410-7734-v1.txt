KREUZNACH, a city of Prussia, the capital of a circle of the same name, in the province of Lower Rhine. It is on the river Nake. It contains 700 houses, and 7205 inhabitants. It has fabrics of leather, snuff, and soap, and very considerable establishments for salt refining, and has a lively trade in wine grown near it, and in oil, potash, corn, spirits, and clover-seed.
