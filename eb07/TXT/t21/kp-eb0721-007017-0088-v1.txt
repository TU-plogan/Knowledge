TAITONG, a city of China, of the first rank, in Shan-see, situated in a mountainous country, and exposed to the incursions of the Tartars. It is well fortified, and has a strong garrison. It is 155 miles west of Pekin. Long. 112. 44. E. Lat. 40. 5. N.
