HUMMOCK Island. There are two small islands of this name, in the Eastern Seas, one fifteen miles south of Mindanao, in long. 126. 37. E. and lat. 5. 24. N. The other, which is inaccessible except at one point, lies in long. 123. 40. E. and lat. 24. 15. N.
