HAL, or Halle, a city of the Netherlands, in the province of South Brabant. It is situated on the river Senne, ten miles from Brussels; and has extensive breweries, distilleries, salt-refineries, paper-mills, and tanneries, with 700 houses, and 4612 inhabitants.
