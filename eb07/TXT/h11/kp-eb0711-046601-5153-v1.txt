HINGHAM, a town of the county of Norfolk, in the hundred of Forehoe, ninety-six miles from London, situated at the source of the river Yare. The land around is fertile, and there is a market on Thursday. The inhabitants amounted in 1801 to 1203, in 1811 to 1311, in 1821 to 1442, and in 1831 to 1539.
