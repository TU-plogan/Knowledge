HENERY Isle, a small island lying due south from Bombay. It is about 600 yards in circumference, and nearly of a circular form. It is fortified and well inhabited. In 1790 it belonged to Ragojee Angria, and was a principal rendezvous of private vessels, though within sight of Bombay.
