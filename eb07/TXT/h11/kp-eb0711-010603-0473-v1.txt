HAGENAU, a city of the department of the Lower Rhine, in the arrondissement of Strasbourg, in France. It is situated on the navigable river Motter, which near it joins the Rhine. It is fortified, but not strongly, and is well built. It contains 900 houses and 7694 inhabitants. There are many small manufactures carried on, and it is a great corn market. Long. 7. 42. E. Lat. 48. 48. N.
