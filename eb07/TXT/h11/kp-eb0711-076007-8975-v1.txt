HURRIAL, a town of Bengal, district of Rajesky, where the East India Company have a commercial residency for the purchase of cloths. The adjoining country is under water during the rainy season, and the town can only at that time be approached in boats. Long. 89. 17. E. Lat. 24. 19. N.
