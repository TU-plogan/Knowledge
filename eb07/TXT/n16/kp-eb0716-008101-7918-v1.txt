NEIDERSELTERS, a town of the bailiwick of Idstein, in the duchy of Nassau, in Germany, celebrated for the mineral spring which yields the water known through Europe by the name of Seltzer water. Besides what is used on the spot, more than two millions and a half of jugs of it are despatched to different countries, and produce a clear revenue of L.5000. The town is situated in a picturesque country, and contains about 900 resident inhabitants, but receives a great deal of company during the summer season.
