NEAMUTSERAI, a fortified village of Afghanistan, in the district of Puckoli, on the north-eastern border of Lahore. It has a caravanserai, which is placed in a break of the great range of mountains, which extends from the Punjab to the Indus. Long. 71. 50. E. Lat. 33. 30. N.
