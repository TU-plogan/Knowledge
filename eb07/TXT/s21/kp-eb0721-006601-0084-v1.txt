SZIGETH, a town of the kingdom of Hungary, in the province of the Farther Theiss, the capital of a circle of the same name. It stands at the influx of the river Itza to the Theiss. It contains a Catholic college, and churches for that sect, one for the Lutherans and one for the Unitarians, with 6850 inhabitants. The greater part of the kingdom is supplied with salt from this place. Long. 24. 16. 25. E. Lat. 47. 56. 8. N.
