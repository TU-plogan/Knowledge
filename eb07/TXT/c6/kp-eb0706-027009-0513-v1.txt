CELASTRUS. In Senegal the negroes use the powder of the root of this plant as a spécifié against gonorrhoeas, whieh it is said to cure in eight, and sometimes in three days. An infusion of the bark of a speeies of staff-tree, which grows in the Isle of Franee, is said to possess the same virtues.
