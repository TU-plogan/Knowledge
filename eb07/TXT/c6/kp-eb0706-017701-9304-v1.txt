CARRION de los Condes, a town of Spain, in the province of Leon. It is situated near the confluence of the rivers Carrion and Ciesa. The eountry around it is fruitful in wine and in eorn; but being wholly destitute of trees, it has a very barren appearance. This town eontains 2800 inhabitants.
