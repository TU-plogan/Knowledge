CELLBRIDGE, or Kiłdrought, a town of Ireland, in the eounty of Kildare. It is situated upon the river Liffey, over whieh there is a fine stone bridge. The town consists chiefly of two broad avenues, intcrseeting each other at right angles, and presenting a regular appearanee. Manufactures of woollen cloths and of chip hats have been occasionally earrĩed on here. The population amounts to about 1300. It is distant fourteen miles west by south from Dublin.
