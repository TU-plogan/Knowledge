CHUDLEIGH, a market-town of the hundred of Ex-minstcr, in Devonshire, 182 miles from London. It is situated on the river Teign, and has considerable woollen manufactures. The market is held on Saturday. The inhabitants amounted in 1811 to 1832, in 1821 to 2053, and in 1831 to 2278.
