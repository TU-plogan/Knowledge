CANISIUS, Henry, a native of Nimeguen, and one of the most learned men of his time, was professor of canon law at Ingoldstadt, and wrote a number of books, the principal of which are, 1. Summa Juris Canonici ; 2. Antiques Lectiones, a very valuable work. He died in 1609.
