CHICKOORY, a large and respectable town of Hindustan, in the territory of the Poonah Mahrattas. It has an extensive bazar, and is pleasantly situated near a rivulet. It has a manufactory of cloth, chiefly for the dress of the country people, and is forty-five miles S.S.W. from Merritch. Long. 74. 50. E. Lat. 16. 23. N.
