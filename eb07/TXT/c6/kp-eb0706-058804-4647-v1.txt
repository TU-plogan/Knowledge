CHINCHILLA, a small city of Spain, in the province of Murcia, containing 4624 inhabitants. It is celebrated on aeeount of a great fair held annually, in a large public building at Albaeetc, a village near to it. The plains of Almanza adjoin it, on which was fought the famous battle whieh established the Bourbons on the throne of Spain. A pyramid raised on the spot stands to commemorate this event. Lat. 38. 48. N.
