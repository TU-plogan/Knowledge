COMILLAH, a town of Bengal, district of Tipperah, of which it is the capital, situated on the south bank of the Goomat river. It is the modern town, the former capital having been Odeypore, which lies thirty miles farther east. It is fifty-one miles south-east from Dacca. Long. 91. 2. E. Lat. 23. 28. N.
