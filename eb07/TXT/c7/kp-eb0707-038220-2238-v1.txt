COSMOLOGY (from κοσμο;, world, and λoyoς, discourse), the seienee of the world in general. This ΛVolfius calls general or transeendental eosmology, and has written a treatise on the subjeet, in whieh he endeavours to explain how the world arose from simple substances, and treats of the general principles, of the modifications of material things, of the elements of bodies, of the laws of motion, of the perfection of the world, and of the order and eourse of nature.
