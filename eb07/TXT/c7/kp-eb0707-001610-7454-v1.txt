CODEX, in Antiquity, denotes a book or tablet on which the ancients wrote.

Codex also denoted a kind of punishment by means of [7:7:17] a log or block of wood, which slaves who had offended were made fast to, and obliged to drag along with them; but sometimes they sat on it closely bound.
