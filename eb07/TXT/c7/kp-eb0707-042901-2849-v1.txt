COULOMMIERS, an arrondissement in the department of the Seine and Marne, in France, extending over 465 square miles, and containing 50,243 inhabitants. The city which gives its name to the arrondissement is on the right bank of the great Morin, and contained 570 houses and 3530 inhabitants.
