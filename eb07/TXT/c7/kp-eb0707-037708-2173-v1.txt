CORSHAM, a town of the hundred of Chippenham, in the county of Wilts, ninety-seven miles from London. It is well built, in a healthy situation. Some broad eloths are made, but to a less extent than formerly. The market is held on Wednesday. The inhabitants amounted in 1811 to 2394, in 1821 to 2727, and in 1831 to 2952.
