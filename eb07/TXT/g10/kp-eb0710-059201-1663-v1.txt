GMUND, a bailiwick of the kingdom of Wurtemberg, in the circle of Jaxt, extending over 121 square miles, and comprehending two cities, one market-town, and 131 villages, with 22,640 inhabitants. It is a mountainous and woody district, not yielding corn equal to the demand. The capital is a city of the same name, situated on the river Rems. It contains 806 houses, and 5875 inhabitants, who are employed in making cloths, hosiery, and other clothing, and many of them in the trade of silversmiths and jewellers. Long. 10. 0. 4. E. Lat. 48. 48. 15. N.
