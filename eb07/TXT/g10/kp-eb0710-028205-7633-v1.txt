GAINAGE, Gainagium, in our ancient writers, signifies the draught oxen, horses, wain, plough, and furniture, for carrying on the work of tillage by the baser sort of Sokemen and villeins.

Gainage is also used to signify the land itself, or the profit raised by cultivating it.
