GOOCHNAUTH, a village of Hindustan, in the province of Gujrat and district of Werrear, situated on the south bank of the Bunass river about three miles southeast frotn Rahdunpoor. The neighbouring country is in a state of high cultivation, and most of the fields are enclosed. It belongs to a Mahommedan chief.
