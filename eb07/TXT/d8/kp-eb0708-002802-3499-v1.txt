DIGLIGGY NEUR, a town in the island of Ceylon, situated among thick woods and mountains ten or twelve miles to the eastward of Candy, in the direction of Batta-colo. When the king was driven out of Candy in 1803 he retreated to this place, to which the European army could not penetrate.
