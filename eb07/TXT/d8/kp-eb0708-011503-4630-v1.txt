DONSKAIA, a fortress of Asiatie Russia, situated on the brook Taschle, the first of a chain of posts established between the Sea of Azof and the Caspian. It contains about forty houses; and adjacent to it is a village of Cossacks. It is 116 miles north-north-west of Ekaterinograd.
