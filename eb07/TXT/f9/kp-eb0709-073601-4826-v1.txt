FOOSHT, an island in the Red Sea, situated, according to the observations of Mr Bruce, in lat. 13. 59. 43. N. It is described by him as about five miles in length from north to south; and it is low and sandy in the southern part, but in the north rises a black hill of inconsiderable height.
