FATESCH, one of the circles into which the Russian province of Kursk is divided. It is a district fertile in corn, watered by the Usoscha, and contains about 46,000 inhabitants. The chief place is of the same name, and has a population of 2450 persons. It is situated in long. 37. 5. E. and lat. 52. 20. N.
