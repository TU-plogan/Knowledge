DELITZSCH, a circle in the Prussian province of Sax-pny, extending over 308 square miles, or 197,120 English acres, comprehending three cities or towns and 174 villages, with 34,514 inhabitants. The capital, of the same name, on the Lober, is a fortified town, containing 499 houses and 2953 inhabitants.
