DELFSHAVEN, a market-town in the circle of Rotterdam, in the Netherlands, with a good harbour on the right bank of the Maas, in which the Delft vessels lie. It carries on great fisheries for herrings and stock-fish, and has large distilleries for gin. The inhabitants amount to 2680.
