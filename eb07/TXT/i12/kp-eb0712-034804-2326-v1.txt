IREG, a market-town of the circle of Sezerem, in the Austrian Sclavonian province of Siebenbirgen. It stands at the foot of the Karlovitz Mountains, in a fruitful district yielding good wine. It contains 1020 houses, with 4863 inhabitants. Long. 19. 47. 59. E. Lat. 45. 6. 47. N.
