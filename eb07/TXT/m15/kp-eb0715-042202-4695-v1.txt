MONTAGNANO, a city of Italy, in the Austrian delegation of Padua. It is situated on the Fiumecello, and is surrounded with walls. It contains a conventual and several other churches, with 8275 inhabitants, who are [7:15:423] occupied in woollen manufactures, in tanneries, and in making hats. It is situated in longitude 11. 25. 30. E. and latitude 45.13. 3. N.
