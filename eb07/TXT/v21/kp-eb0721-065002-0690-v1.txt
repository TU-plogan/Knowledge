VILLAFRANCA, a small town in the delegation of Verona in Venetian Lombardy, with a population of 2000. It is nine miles distant from Verona.

Villafranca, a town of Spain, in the province of Estremadura, distant from Madrid about 200 miles. It is a place of but little importance, and has a population of about 2500.
