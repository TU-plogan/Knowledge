EHUD, the son of Gera, a Benjamite, a left-handed man, who delivered Israel from the oppression of Eglon, king of Moab, under whom they served for eighteen years. This will be found recorded in the third chapter of Judges.

El A, or Ey, in our old writers, is used to indicate an island. Hence the names of places ending in ey denote that they are islands. Thus, Ramsey, the isle of rams; Shepey, the isle of sheep; and so of others.
