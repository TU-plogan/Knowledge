LIPPA, a fortified town of the circle of Arad, in the province of Farther Theiss, in Hungary. It stands on the river Maros, has a Catholic and a Greek church, and is the seat of a protopope. It contains 390 houses, and 2530 inhabitants, who make much culinary salt, and grow good corn and wine. Long. 21. 35. 7. E. Lat. 46. 5. 9. N.
