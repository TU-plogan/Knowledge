LEOBEN, a city of Austria, in the circle of Bruch, in the province of Steycrmark. It stands on the river Mur, and is the see of a bishop; contains 278 houses, and 2200 inhabitants, who are employed in making iron. It is remarkable for the peace concluded at it in 1798, between Austria and France. Long. 15. 0. 25. E. Lat. 47. 23. 36. N.
