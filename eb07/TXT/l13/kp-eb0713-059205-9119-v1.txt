LUDEHAUNAH, a town of Hindustan, situated on the north-west boundary of the province of Delhi, and on the south bank of the Sutlege river. It is one of the most remote military stations which the British possess in this quarter. The climate is cold for four months of the year, and in the summer season extremely hot, with hot winds. The rains are abundant. It is 180 miles north-north-west from the city of Delhi, and 120 south-east from Lahore. Long. 75. 32. E. Lat. 36. 53. N.
