LINEAR Numbers, in Mathematics, such as have relation to length only; like a number which represents one side of a plain figure. If the plain figure be a square, the linear figure is called a root. Linear Problem, that which may be solved geometrically by the intersection of two right lines. This is called a simple problem, and is capable of but one solution.
