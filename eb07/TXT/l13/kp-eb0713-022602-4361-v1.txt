LEMNIAN Earth, Terra Lemnia, a medicinal, astringent sort of Oar th, of a fatty consistence and reddish colour. It derives its name from the island of Lemnos, whence it is chiefly brought. Many form it into round cakes, and impress a seal upon it; and hence it is also called terra sigillata.
