PATCHOW Islands, a group of small islands, situated on the coast of China. The easternmost is Typinsan, having on its north side an extensive reef, on which the Providence, Captain Broughton, was wrecked in 1797, in long. 125.11. E. Lat. 25. 6. N. The southernmost island is in long. 123. 52. E. and lat. 24. 6. N.
