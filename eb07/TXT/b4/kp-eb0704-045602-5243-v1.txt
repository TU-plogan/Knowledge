BAUMAN Islands, a cluster of islands in the South Pacific Ocean, discovered in 1722 by Bauman, who accompanied the Dutch navigator Roggeweji round the world. The inhabitants are said to be friendly to strangers. Long 173. W. Lat. 12. S.
