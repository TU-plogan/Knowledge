BENDING, in a general sense, the reducing of a straight body into a curve, or giving it a crooked form.

Bending, in the sea language, the tying of two ropes or cables together; thus, to bend the cable, is to make it fast to the ring of the anchor; to bend the sail, to make it fast to the yard.
