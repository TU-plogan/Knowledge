BENDER, a town of Bessarabia in European Russia, seated on the river Dniester. It is remarkable for being the place of retreat of Charles XII. after he was defeated by the Russians at the battle of Pultowa in 1709. Long. 29. 36. E. Lat. 46. 50. 32. N.
