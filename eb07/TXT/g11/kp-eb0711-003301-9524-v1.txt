GUJUNDERGUR, a town of Hindustan, in the province of Bejapoor, capital of a district of the same name, which is situated principally between the 15th and 16th degrees of north latitude. It is sixty miles east by north from Darwar. Long. 75. 56. E. Lat. 15. 45. N.
