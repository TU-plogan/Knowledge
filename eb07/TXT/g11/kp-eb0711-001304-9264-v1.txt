GUARANTEE, or Warrantee, in Law, a term relative to warrant or warranter, and properly signifying him whom the warranter undertakes to indemnify or secure from damage.

Guarantee is more frequently used for a warranter, or a person who undertakes and obliges himself to see a second person perform what he has stipulated to a third.
