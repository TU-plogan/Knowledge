JELISAWETGRAD, a city of Russia, in the province of Cherson, the capital of a circle of the same name. It stands on a beautiful plain on the banks of the river Ingul. It is fortified, and defended by a strong citadel. It contains 1500 houses, and 12,000 inhabitants, consisting of various tribes and countries, attracted by large fairs held there in the autumn. Long. 32. 22. E. Lat, 48. 30. N.
