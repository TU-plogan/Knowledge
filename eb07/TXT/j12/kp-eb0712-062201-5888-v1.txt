JORJAN, a town of Persia, in the province of Astrabad, situated to the east of the Caspian. It is considered as one of the strongest fortresses in the kingdom, and is frequently mentioned in Persian history. It is 100 miles west of Meshid, and 300 north-north-east of Ispahan.
