SHAPOORAH, a town of Hindustan, in the province of Ajmeer and district of Harowty. It is a large and well-built town, surrounded by a strong wall of stone and a ditch. It is sixty-five miles south by east from the city of Ajmeer. Long. 75. 9. E. Lat. 25. 43. N.
