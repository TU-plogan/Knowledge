SEYNE, La, a city of France, in the department of the Var, and the arrondissement of Toulon. It is a port on a small bay or roadstead, about three miles from Toulon. It is well built, has good quays, and an establishment for building vessels. It contains 5230 inhabitants, many of whom are occupied in fishing for sardinias and tunnies.
