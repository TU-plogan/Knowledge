MAXIMUM, in Mathematics, denotes the greatest quantity attainable in any given case.

If a quantity conceived to be generated by motion increases or decreases till it arrives at a certain magnitude or position, and then, on the contrary, grow s greater or less, and it be required to determine the said magnitude or position, the question is called a problem de maximis et minimis.
