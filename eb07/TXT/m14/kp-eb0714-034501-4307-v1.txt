MEASURING, or Mensuration, is the using a certain known measure, for determining thereby the precise extent, quantity, or capacity of any thing.

Measuring, in general, includes the practical part of geometry. From the various subjects on which it is employed, it acquires various names, and constitutes various arts. See Geometry, Levelling, Mensuration, Trigonometry.
