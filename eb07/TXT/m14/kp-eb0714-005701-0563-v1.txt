MALICE, in Ethics and Law, is a deliberate preconceived design of doing mischief to another; and hence it differs from hatred. In murder it is malice that constitutes the crime; and if a man, having a malicious intent to kill another, in the execution of his malice kills a person not intended, the malice will be connected to his person, and he will be adjudged a murderer. âˆ•
