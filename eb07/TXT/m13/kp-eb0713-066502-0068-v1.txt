MACROBII, a people of Ethiopia, celebrated for their ustice, and the innocence of their manners; also a people n the island of Meroe. The Hyperboreans were likewise palled Macrobii. They generally lived to their hundred and -wentieth year, and from their longevity they obtained heir name (i u,ακgoς βιος, long life).
